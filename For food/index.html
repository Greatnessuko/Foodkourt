Organic Foods

Our organic food is grown without the use of synthetic chemicals, such as human-made pesticides and fertilizers, and does not contain genetically modified organisms (GMOs). Our organic foods include fresh produce, meats, and dairy products as well as processed foods such as cakes, drinks, and frozen meals.

Quality Meals

Our Food quality is based on the organoleptic characteristics (e.g., taste, aroma, appearance) and nutritional value of food. We ensure that our meals meet international standards that offers customer satisfaction as well as maintaining food safety rules. We don't just cook, we take care of your health and taste.

Fresh Meals

Our daily cooked fresh meals do not come from old preservations and has not spoiled. For our pastries and cakes, we bake them at our requests, to preserve taste and quality; Our meats, are recently slaughtered and butchered; and for our fish, they are recently caught or harvested and kept cold.

On Time Delivery

Our on time delivery, is the metric we use to measure our supply chain efficiency. This shows us whether we are meeting our goals in regards to promised delivery times, or not and this is critical to us for measuring and maintaining customer satisfaction. Once you book a meal, we ensure to deliver within the agreed time window, seamlessly and effortlessly.

Low fat products

For every 100 calories of our meals, foods, pastries or products, we ensure it has 3 grams of fat or less. This puts us within the international standard practice of delivering low-fat products. This means 30% or less of the calories come from fat and totally harmless to your health.

About Food Court's Founder

Peace Cletus is the creator, owner and operator of Food Courts. The passion for clean and healthy food brought about the incorporation of more raw foods into today's meals. When I shopped for food, I noticed there were few offerings of prepared raw meals, and all the raw snacks available were filled with too much sugar (dates, honey, & high fructose fruits). As a Chemist, my desire to help people make better food choices has been my mission for over 3 years now. As a result, I was inspired to create high quality prepared foods and snacks that are minimally processed, easy to digest, and taste awesome. Thus, the idea for Food Court was born. I started Food Court in September 2020 in a small commercial kitchen, with the goal of providing delicious raw, organic, gluten-free food to the public. In December 2022, I moved to a much larger facility to better serve the growing demand.
